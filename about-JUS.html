About Jamacada

maxay qabataa jaamacada umada soomaaliyed?

Jaamacadani waxay leedahay kuliyado tayo sare leh sida: Kuliyada caafimaadka, engineering, ICT, culuum diineed, iyo social science.

kuliyadaha ay ka koobantahy

kuliyaheeda waxaa kamid ah engineering, science, vectanry, ecnomic, beeraha, low, medicin

waa sidee waxbarashadeeda jamacad

waxbarashadeeda aadbeey ufiicantahy marka loo ego jaamacdaha wadanka ee aadka ufiican waxayna leedahay shahaado caalami ah oo hadii aad kaqaadato ad caalamaka kumari karto meelkasta

ma xiiseyneysaa jamacdeyda ina kusoo biirtaa?

haa dabcan sababto ah jaamacadaan lamid ma aha jaamacadakale ee wadanka qofkii raba inuu kusoobiraa waa inuu exam soogalaa macalin iyo ardaybo taasna waxay cadeeynee in aqoonteeda aad usareyso waa nasiib badantahy hadey ku aqbasho jamacda.
